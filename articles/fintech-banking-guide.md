One of the biggest challenges for international entrepreneurs is setting up a business bank account. Traditional high street banks often require physical presence, which isn't always feasible.

## The Fintech Solution

Modern fintech platforms offer a robust alternative, providing multi-currency accounts, low fees, and rapid setup times. They are perfect for global businesses.

## Top Platforms

### Wise (formerly TransferWise)
Excellent for multi-currency transactions and transparent exchange rates.

### Revolut Business
Offers powerful spend management tools and crypto capabilities.

### Airwallex
Great for e-commerce businesses needing to collect payments globally.
