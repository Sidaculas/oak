Starting a business in the UK as a non-resident is simpler than you might think. With the right guidance, you can have your UK Limited Company up and running in as little as 24 hours.

## Why Choose the UK?

The UK offers one of the most stable and prestigious business environments in the world. It is a global financial hub with a transparent legal system and a business-friendly tax regime.

## Key Requirements

1.  **Company Name**: Must be unique and not offensive.
2.  **Director**: At least one director (can be non-resident).
3.  **Shareholder**: At least one shareholder (can be the same as director).
4.  **Registered Office**: Must be a physical address in the UK (we provide this).
